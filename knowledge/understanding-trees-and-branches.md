# Understanding Trees and Branches

Firefly Grove uses a natural metaphor to organize memories: Trees represent people, Branches organize their stories by theme, and Memories are the fireflies that glow within.

## Why This Structure?

### Person-First Organization

Unlike chronological timelines or random albums, Firefly Grove organizes by **who**, not **when**.

This matches how we naturally remember:
- âŒ "What happened on June 12, 2019?"
- âœ… "What stories do I have about Grandma?"

### Flexible Themes

Branches let you organize however makes sense:
- **By topic**: Recipes, Wisdom, Stories, Career
- **By time**: Childhood, College Years, Retirement
- **By location**: The Farm, Beach House, Road Trips
- **By relationship**: As a Mother, As a Friend, As a Mentor

### Scalable

- 10 trees â†’ 30 branches â†’ 500 memories

The structure scales without becoming overwhelming.

## Trees vs Open Grove

**Your Grove (Private Trees)**
- People you have personal connections to
- You control all memories and branches
- Private by default

**Open Grove (Shared Legacy Trees)**
- Public historical figures or shared family trees
- Community-contributed memories
- Anyone can add (with moderation)

## How to Get Started

### Creating Your First Tree

1. Go to your Grove dashboard
2. Click an empty tree slot
3. Enter the person's name
4. Choose "Living" or "Legacy" tree
5. Click "Plant Tree"

### Adding Your First Branch

1. Open the tree you just created
2. Click "Plant a Branch"
3. Name it something meaningful (e.g., "Her Kitchen Wisdom")
4. Add a description (optional)
5. Click "Create Branch"

### Adding Your First Memory

1. Open the branch you created
2. Click "Add Memory" or use Story Sparks for prompts
3. Write, record audio, or upload photos
4. Click "Save Memory"

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I move memories between branches?**

A: Yes! Open the memory, click the menu, and select "Move to another branch."

**Q: How many branches should I create per tree?**

A: Start with 3-5 broad themes. You can always split them later as you add more memories.

**Q: Can I rename or delete branches?**

A: Yes. Open the branch settings (âš™ï¸ icon) to rename, edit description, or delete. Deleting a branch also deletes its memories, so be careful!

**Q: What's the difference between a Living Tree and Legacy Tree?**

A: It's mainly semantic. Legacy trees are for people who have passed. Living trees are for people still with us. You can convert between them anytime.

**Q: Can I share specific branches with family?**

A: Yes! Each branch has sharing settings. You can invite family to view or contribute to specific branches.
