# Choosing Your Keepers

Your memories hold light that shouldn't fade when you're gone. Choosing keepers ensures your stories find the right hands at the right time.

---

## What Are Keepers?

Keepers (also called "heirs") are the people you designate to receive your memories after you pass. They become the guardians of your stories, ensuring the light you've captured continues to glow for generations.

Think of them as trustees of your digital legacy ‚Äî the ones you trust to preserve, share, and honor your memories.

## How to Choose Keepers

### Step-by-Step

1. **Open Branch Settings**

   Navigate to any branch and click the settings icon (‚öôÔ∏è) in the top right.

2. **Go to the Heirs Tab**

   You'll see tabs at the top: Details, Sharing, **Heirs**. Click Heirs.

3. **Add a Keeper**

   Click "Add Heir" and enter their email address. You can add multiple keepers for each branch.

4. **Set Release Conditions**

   Choose when your keeper receives access:
   - **After Passing**: Access granted when you're confirmed deceased
   - **Specific Date**: Access on a predetermined date (birthdays, anniversaries)
   - **Immediate**: Access right away (for shared family archives)

5. **Add a Personal Message (Optional)**

   Write a note that your keeper will see when they receive access. Something like:

   *"These are memories of my grandmother. I hope they help you know her as I did."*

6. **Save Your Choices**

   Your keepers are now set. They won't be notified until release conditions are met.

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I change keepers after I've set them?**

A: Yes, absolutely. You can add, remove, or change keepers anytime. Your current choices aren't locked in.

**Q: Will my keepers know I've chosen them?**

A: Not unless you tell them. Keepers aren't notified until the release conditions are met. This gives you privacy to plan without obligation.

**Q: What if my keeper doesn't use Firefly Grove?**

A: They'll receive an email invitation when release conditions are met. They can create a free account to access your memories. No subscription required for heirs.

**Q: Can I have different keepers for different trees or branches?**

A: Yes! Each branch can have its own set of keepers. This lets you match memories with the most meaningful recipients.

**Q: What happens if I don't choose any keepers?**

A: Your memories will remain in your account, accessible only to you. They won't be deleted, but they also won't be passed on automatically.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many keepers I can have?**

A: You can add up to 10 keepers per branch. For most people, 2-3 trusted keepers per branch is plenty.
