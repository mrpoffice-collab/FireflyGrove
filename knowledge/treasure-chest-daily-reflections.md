# Treasure Chest: Daily Reflections

Capture one moment worth remembering, every day. Build a glow trail of small treasures that, together, tell the story of your life.

---

## What Is Treasure Chest?

Treasure Chest is your daily reflection practice. Each day, you're prompted to capture one thing:

- A moment worth remembering
- Something you're grateful for
- A small observation
- A feeling or realization
- A funny thing that happened

These aren't full memories â€” they're quick captures. Think of them as:
- **Journal entry** (a few sentences)
- **Photo note** (a picture with caption)
- **Voice memo** (30-second reflection)

Over time, these daily treasures become a rich tapestry of your everyday life.

The big events are easy to remember. It's the small moments that fade:
- The way sunlight hit the kitchen counter
- Your daughter's joke at breakfast
- The perfect cup of coffee
- A stranger's kindness

Treasure Chest helps you notice and preserve these fleeting moments.

## Grace Tokens: Never Lose Your Streak

Life happens. You forget, get busy, or travel without internet.

**Grace tokens** let you save your streak:

- You get **2 grace tokens per month**
- Use a token to "fill in" a missed day retroactively
- Write a quick reflection for yesterday
- Your streak continues unbroken
- Tokens refresh on the 1st of each month

**When to use grace:**
- You were sick and forgot
- Traveling and had no internet
- Just realized at midnight you didn't capture today's treasure

**Pro tip:** Don't waste grace on early days. A 3-day streak breaking isn't tragic. Save tokens for when your streak is 50+ days.

## Common Questions

**Q: What's the difference between a treasure and a regular memory?**

A: Treasures are quick daily captures. Memories are fuller stories. Think of treasures as journal entries, memories as photo album pages with full context.

**Q: Can I go back and add treasures for past days?**

A: Only with grace tokens, and only for the previous 2-3 days. Treasure Chest is about present awareness, not retroactive documentation.

**Q: Can I edit treasures after saving them?**

A: Yes! Click on any treasure in your history to edit or expand it.

**Q: Do treasures count toward my storage limit?**

A: Yes, but they're typically very small (text is negligible, short audio is small, photos count normally).

**Q: Can I skip the prompt and just write what I want?**

A: Absolutely. The prompt is a suggestion, not a requirement.

**Q: What if I have nothing interesting to say?**

A: That's fine. "Quiet day. Nothing special. Felt peaceful." is a valid treasure. Years from now, even ordinary days will be precious.
